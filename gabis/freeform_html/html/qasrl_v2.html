Question Answering


Annotating this HIT for the first time?
Follow this short presentation!

Task Definition
You are given a sentence with a predefined target word. Your task is to ask simple questions related to the word which could be answered from the sentence.
Ask as many questions as you can
Note: In some instances, you might not find any questions to ask. If that's the case, press the "I can't ask about this word!" button.

Asking Questions
Find the marked word in the sentence, and use the radio buttons to ask questions about it.
Once you finish phrasing the question, click on "add", and start phrasing a new question.
If you wish to re-ask a question, click "Undo".

Finishing Up
When you finished creating all the possible questions, click "Submit".

Above all, use your common sense. Good luck!
