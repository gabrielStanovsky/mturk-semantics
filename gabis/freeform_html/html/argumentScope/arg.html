Question Answering


Task Definition
You are given a sentence with a predefined target word, and a predefined answer.
Your task is to ask a simple question which corresponds to the given word and predefined answer. For example:

   Sentence:     Obama, the president of the United States, visited Canada on Wednesday.  
   Answer 1:     Obama, the president of the United States  
  Who visited somewhere?   ✔    
  When did someone visit somewhere?   ✘   This question does not correspond to the answer

   Answer 2:     Canada  
  Where did someone visit?   ✔    
  Who did someone visit?   ✘   This question does not correspond to the answer

Asking Questions
Find the target word, marked in blue within the sentence.
Find the current answer, marked in yellow, and also written below the question.
Finally, use the drop-down menus to ask the corresponding question, and click "submit"

Above all, use your common sense. Good luck!
