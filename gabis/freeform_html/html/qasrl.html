Question Answering


Task Definition
You are given a sentence with a predefined target word. Your task is to ask and answer simple questions related to the word, using other words from the sentence.
Ask as many questions as you can, and make sure that your answers are as shortest as possible, while containing all of the needed information. For example:

   Sentence 1:     Obama, the president of the United States, visited Canada on Wednesday.  
  Who visited somewhere?   ✔   Obama    
  Who visited somewhere?   ✘   Obama, the president of the United States   There is a shorter answer  
  Where did someone visit?   ✔   Canada    
  When did someone visit?   ✔   on Wednesday   The answer should include the preposition ("on") if it doesn't appear in the question  

   Sentence 2:     "Obama and Clinton were born in America"  
  Who was born somewhere?   ✔   Obama    
  Who was born somewhere?   ✔   Clinton         Split answers when you can  

   Sentence 3:     Obama and Clinton met to discuss options for reducing global warming and carbon emissions over the next five years.  
  Who met?   ✔   Obama and Clinton    
  Who met?   ✘   Clinton   This answer provides only partial information  
  Why did someone meet?   ✔   to discuss options for reducing global warming
and carbon emissions over the next five years		    
  Why did someone meet?   ✘   to discuss options   Make sure to fully answer the question with all the information from the sentence  

Asking and Answering
Find the marked word in the sentence, and use the drop-down menus to ask questions about it.
Once you finish phrasing the question, click on words from the sentence to form the appropriate answer (click the first an last word in the span).
When you finish answering, click the "Answer" button.
In case you can't find any question to ask about the target word, just click the "I can't ask about this word!" button.

Finishing Up
When you have finished creating all the possible questions and answers, click "Submit".
Note that words used to answer previous questions cannot be reused to answer new ones. If you wish to re-ask or re-answer a question, click "Undo".

Above all, use your common sense. Good luck!
